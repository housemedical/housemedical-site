Careers at House Medical

Join our mission to elevate healthcare across the UK through innovative consulting and tailored product solutions. We value collaboration, integrity, and a commitment to excellence. If you are passionate about healthcare and want to make a meaningful impact, explore our current openings below.

Healthcare Consultant Associate

Support client engagements focusing on healthcare strategy, operational efficiency, and regulatory compliance. Perform detailed data collection, contribute to analytical assessments, and assist senior consultants in developing data-driven recommendations. Collaborate with teams to implement solutions and monitor outcomes.

Requirements:

  • At least two years of relevant experience in healthcare consulting or related fields
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Familiarity with NHS operations and the UK healthcare regulatory environment
  • Proficiency in data analysis tools (e.g., Excel, Tableau)

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary
  • Professional development and training opportunities
  • Potential for flexible work arrangements
  • Scope for career progression within a growing team

  • Basic proficiency in CRM software and Microsoft Office Suite

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary

  • Access to professional training and development resources
  • Opportunities for flexible working options
